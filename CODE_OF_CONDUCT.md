# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

redis-tui is a welcoming project. All participants are expected to treat each other with respect in issues, pull requests, and any other project spaces.

**Expected behaviour:**
- Be constructive and patient in code review and discussion
- Accept feedback graciously; give feedback on the code, not the person
- Welcome contributors of all experience levels

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment of any kind
- Deliberately hostile or dismissive responses

## Enforcement

Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments or ban participants who repeatedly violate these standards.
